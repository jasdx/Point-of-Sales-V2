<b>⚡ JazzPOS: Retail Pro V2.0</b>

JazzPOS is a modern, high-performance Point of Sale (POS) system designed for retail excellence. Built with a focus on intuitive user experience and clean aesthetics, it provides business owners with real-time insights, effortless inventory management, and a streamlined sales process.

✨ Features

📊 Comprehensive Dashboard

Get a high-level overview of your business at a glance.

Real-time Metrics: Track Today's Revenue, Total Sales, and Items in Stock.

Low Stock Alerts: Automatic visual indicators for items needing replenishment.

Transaction History: Monitor recent sales activity directly from the main screen.

Top Selling Products: Quick view of your most popular inventory items.

📦 Robust Inventory Management

Maintain full control over your product catalog with a beautiful, organized interface.

Product Tracking: Detailed views including SKU, Category, and Price.

Visual Status: Color-coded stock levels (Red alerts for low stock).

CRUD Operations: Easily add, edit, or delete products to keep your store current.

⚙️ Personalized Settings

Tailor the system to fit your specific business needs.

Store Identity: Customize business name and currency symbols.

Tax Configuration: Set global tax rates for accurate billing.

System Maintenance: Perform factory resets to clear data for new cycles.

Dark Mode Support: Switch between themes for comfortable viewing in any lighting.

🎨 Design Philosophy

JazzPOS utilizes a "Glassmorphism" inspired design language with:

Soft UI Elements: Rounded corners and subtle shadows for a premium feel.

High Contrast: Vibrant purple accents against a clean, neutral background for better accessibility.

Responsive Navigation: A persistent sidebar for lightning-fast switching between the Terminal, Inventory, and Analytics.
